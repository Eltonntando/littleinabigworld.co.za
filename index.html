All About The Little in a Big World Project

Little Big World is a project aimed at children for educating them about the world they live in. We have published a Children Story book titled Little in a Big Word, and it's a book consisting of seven (7) engaging, inspirational, and educational stories about being little but living in a big world with a lot going on at once. Children will learn a lot by just reading the stories as they touch many different areas of their lives and also how to deal with circumstances they find themselves in. This book will teach kids about even the planet they live in together with some knowledge about even the solar system etc.

Research and surveys shows that children learn a lot better and quicker when assisted by colourful content as it also enhances their visual senses altogether with their imagination, helping them become more creative by also enhancing their intuition which is a very handy tool when growing up and everyday learning. Also reading story books to little kids helps them learn how to talk even quicker and does even much better for older kids by teaching them language enhancing their vocabulary, prepositions, tenses, and more.
